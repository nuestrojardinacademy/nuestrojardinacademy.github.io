Originally from Venezuela, Nelly arrived in the United States 2 years ago. In her home country, she never had the chance to pursue a degree in education, as she became a mother at a young age, but she knew she always wanted to work with children.

She is excited for the opportunity to work at Nuestro Jardín as a floating and support teacher, where she can teach children her native language and also learn about the Reggio Emilia approach.

Nelly is an avid baker, and in her free time, she’s in the kitchen baking something delicious with her 6 grandchildren!
