{% trans "Detailed class descriptions" %}

{% trans "The Wonders of Nature – Weeks 1-4" %}

{% trans "Children will explore the connections between nature and art through movement, crafts and outdoor play. Natural materials such as seeds, flowers, rocks and sticks will be used to create original works of art that reflect children’s ideas and experiences. The curriculum will emerge based upon shared interests of the children. The goal of the camp is to inspire a love and appreciation for the natural world." %}

{% trans "Our Environment and Us – Weeks 5-8" %}

{% trans "Children will explore ways to connect to our environment and each other through daily walks around our community, movement, arts, crafts, and outdoor play. The outdoors will become a science lab where children will use natural materials to represent their ideas. Each week will be focused on a specific theme, based on shared interests of the children. The goal of the camp is twofold: to instill children's curiosity for the outdoors and to discover their own ways to connect to their environment." %}
